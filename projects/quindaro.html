Abstract

This Land Management Plan was created as a capstone project for the University of Kansas Environmental Studies Program. It was developed concurrently with a companion Cultural Landscape Assessment (Brown, 2023), which should be consulted alongside this document for a complete picture of the site's cultural and historical significance. This plan is designed for the Quindaro Ruins Project Foundation and its partners, including site planners, land managers, and preservation stakeholders.

The objectives of this land management plan are to assess the Quindaro Ruins site from an ecological and archaeological standpoint. Specifically, this plan: (1) provides a brief overview of the ecology and history of the Quindaro Township; (2) identifies goals for land development; (3) assesses the site's environmental and ecological values; (4) identifies environmental threats; (5) examines the condition of ruins and structures; (6) presents options for invasive honeysuckle and vegetation removal; (7) outlines trail implementation; and (8) discusses potential for recreational park development.
